# My Favorite Series: Breaking Bad

**Breaking Bad** is a critically acclaimed drama series that follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher, into one of the most notorious figures in the drug world.


- The character development is deep and realistic.  
- The story becomes more intense with each season.  
- The cinematography and music are outstanding.  
- It explores moral choices and consequences in a compelling way.


- **Walter White** – A chemistry teacher turned drug manufacturer.  
- **Jesse Pinkman** – A former student and partner of Walter.  
- **Skyler White** – Walter’s wife who becomes involved in his secret life.  
- **Hank Schrader** – A DEA agent and Walter’s brother-in-law.


> “I am not in danger, Skyler. I am the danger.”  
> — Walter White
